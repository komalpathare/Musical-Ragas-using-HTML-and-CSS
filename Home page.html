ALL ABOUT RAGAS
A raga or raag is one of the melodic modes used in traditional South Asian genres such as Indian classical music and qawwali.The word raga is derived from a Sanskrit word which means act of coloring or dyeing.A raga uses a series of five to nine musical notes upon which a melody is constructed.In the Indian musical tradition,ragas are associated with different times a day,or with seasons.Indian classical music is always set in a raga.
Non-classical music such as popular Indian film songs and ghazals sometimes use ragas in their compositions.
Pandit Jasraj describes the meaning of raga as "love".
