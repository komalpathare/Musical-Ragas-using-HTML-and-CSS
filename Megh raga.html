Megh Raga

This is one of the very old ragas found in the Indian Classical Music system. This raga is one of Prime Raga in six ragas and 36 raginis.
Related ragas: Ragas of Malhar family, namely Megh Malhar, Miyan ki Malhar, Gaud Malhar, Ramdasi Malhar, Dhuliya Malhar, etc. as well as Madhmad Sarang. Raga Megh is commonly associated with the monsoon season. 

Thaat:Kafi
Aaroha:S m R m P n S
Avroha:S' n P m R m 'n R S
Pakad:R - R - S - 'n - S - m - R - P - m - R - 'n - S
Vaadi:Sa
Samvaadi:Pa -Re
Prahar (Time):Late night.
