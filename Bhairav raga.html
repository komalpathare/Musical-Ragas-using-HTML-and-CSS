Bhairav Raga

Raag Bhairav is a Hindustani Classical hepatonic(Sampurna) Raag of Bhairav Thaat. Traditionally it is a morning raga.In modern times,typically in Khyal Gayaki,it is usually performed as the beginning piece in concerts.It is the defining raga of its own Thaat. Raag Bhairav is typically performed with a peaceful,serious and serene mood.

Thaat:Bhairav
Aaroha:Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni Sa'
Avroha:Sa' Ni Dha Pa Ma Ga Re Sa
Pakad:Ga Ma Dha Dha Pa Ga Ma Re Re Sa
Vaadi:Dha
Samvaadi:Re
Prahar (Time):Morning (Pratham Prahar)
