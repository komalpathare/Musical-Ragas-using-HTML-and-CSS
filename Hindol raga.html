Hindol Raga

Hindol is a Hindustani classical raga. It is an ancient raga addociated with spring season. The raga is to be sung or played on an instrument such as santoor during the first part of the day. The raga has Teevra Madhyam at its heart, and revolves around that note, resting on Dha or Ga.
A prominent movement in Hindol is the gamak, heavy and forceful oscillations particularly using Ma# and Dha. The Ni in the avarohana is very weak, and in most compositions it is used obliquely or often entirely avoided. Mostly pure classical genre of music like Khayals or Dhamars are composed in this raga. 

Thaat:Kalyan
Aaroha:Sa Ga Ma# Dha Ni Dha Sa
Avroha:Sa Ni Dha Ma# Ga Sa
Pakad:Sa Ga Ma# Dha Ni Dha Ma# Ga Sa
Vaadi:Dha
Samvaadi:Ga
Prahar (Time):(12 Night - 3 AM) 3rd Prahar of the Night
