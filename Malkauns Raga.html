Malkaush Raga

Malkauns is a raga in Indian classical music.It is one of the oldest ragas of Indian classical music. The name Malkaush is derived from the combination of Mal and Kaushik,which means he who wears serpents like garlands-the god Shiva.The raga is believed to have been created by goddess Parvati(the wife of Shiva)to calm Shiva,when the lord Shiva was outraged and was not calming down after Tandav in range of Sati's sacrifice. 

Thaat:Bhairavi
Aaroha:.Ni. Sa Ga Ma Dha Ni *Sa*
Avroha:*Sa* Ni Dha Ma Ga Ma Ga Sa OR *Sa* Ni Dha Ma Ga Sa
Pakad:Ga Ma Dha Dha Pa Ga Ma Re Re Sa
Vaadi:Ma
Samvaadi:Sa
Prahar (Time):Late night
