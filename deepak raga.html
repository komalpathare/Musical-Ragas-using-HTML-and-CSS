Deepak Raga

Raga Dipaka,is a musical mode which fire defines as if defines a 'dipaka' in lamp. Both 'dipaka' and raga dipaka justify their names only when they have fire emitting from their bodies.Similarly,love is raga dipaka's pivotal sentiment for love.It is commonly believed that when the Raga Dipaka was sung even in its less potential form,lamps were automatically lit. 

Thaat:Bilawal
Aaroha:Sa Ga Ma Pa' Dha Ni Sa 
Avroha:Sa Ni Dha Pa Ma Ga Re Sa
Vaadi:Ga
Samvaadi:Ni
Prahar (Time):Second half of the night
